About Me

Designing novel experiences.

I’m Braedyn, a UX design student, graphic artist, and amateur musician. I was never comfortable in the box so I learnt to live outside of it. I care about interfaces that feel a little too alive, experiences that poke at norms, and digital spaces not yet seen before.

I help people find new directions to go when they feel stuck.

Outside of pixels and prototypes, you’ll probably find me collecting oddly specific screenshots, overthinking information layout, or redesigning something that technically works but could be way more fun.
